## 4. Evidence Standards

All evidence entered in this log must meet the following standards before a blocker may move from `PENDING EVIDENCE` to `EVIDENCE COLLECTED`.

| Standard | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Written | No verbal confirmations accepted. Evidence must be a document, screenshot, test log, or signed note. |
| Dated | Every piece of evidence must include the date it was completed or captured. |
| Attributed | Every piece of evidence must identify the confirming role or person by name. |
| Specific | Evidence must reference the specific asset, system, channel, or check being confirmed — not a blanket statement. |
| Accessible | Evidence must be stored in a location that can be referenced in the "Evidence link or screenshot reference" field. |
