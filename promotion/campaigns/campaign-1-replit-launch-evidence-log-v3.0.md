## 3. How to Use This Evidence Log

1. Work through blockers in dependency order (Stage 1 → Stage 2 → Stage 3 → Stage 4 → Stage 5) as defined in the Launch Blocker Resolution Plan v3.0 Section 8.
2. For each blocker, collect the required evidence as defined in the Blocker Resolution Plan Section 7.
3. Fill in every field for each blocker: Status, Evidence link or screenshot reference, Test notes, Confirmed by, Date confirmed.
4. Change Status from `PENDING EVIDENCE` to `EVIDENCE COLLECTED` only when written, dated, attributed evidence is on file. Do not mark `CONFIRMED` or `RESOLVED` in this log — those statuses belong to the Revised Launch Readiness Report.
5. Complete the Manual Test Checklist in Section 6 before marking delivery-related blockers as EVIDENCE COLLECTED.
6. When all 14 blockers show `EVIDENCE COLLECTED` and the Evidence Completion Summary (Section 12) is fully populated, the log may be submitted for review against the criteria in Section 13.
7. Do not submit the Revised Launch Readiness Report until this log is complete and reviewed by Trevor Seitz.

**Status definitions:**

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `PENDING EVIDENCE` | No evidence collected yet. Blocker is unresolved. |
| `IN PROGRESS` | Evidence collection started but not complete. |
| `EVIDENCE COLLECTED` | All required evidence is written, dated, and attributed. Ready for inclusion in Revised LRR review. |

## 4. Evidence Standards

All evidence entered in this log must meet the following standards before a blocker may be moved from `PENDING EVIDENCE` to `EVIDENCE COLLECTED`.

| Standard | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Written | No verbal confirmations. Evidence must be a document, screenshot, test log, or signed note. |
| Dated | Every piece of evidence must include the date it was completed or captured. |
| Attributed | Every piece of evidence must identify who confirmed or completed it by name and role. |
| Specific | Evidence must reference the specific asset, system, channel, or check being confirmed — not a blanket statement. |
| Accessible | Evidence must be stored in a location that can be referenced in the "Evidence link or screenshot reference" field below. |
