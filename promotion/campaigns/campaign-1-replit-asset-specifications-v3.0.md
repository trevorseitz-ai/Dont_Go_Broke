## 6. Asset-by-Asset Specifications

### A-01 — Replit Pamphlet (PDF Lead Magnet)

**Purpose:** Primary lead magnet. Delivers cost-awareness education to first-time Replit AI builders in exchange for an email address. Validates the runaway-cost-fear hypothesis.

**Required inputs:**
- Approved Architecture: Section 7 (Hypothesis), Section 9 (Angle), Section 10 (Primary Message), Section 11 (Supporting Messages), Section 16 (Objections)
- Brand Position: Core Promise, Brand Character, Supporting Messages, Strategic Constraints
- Promotion Brief: Unique Advantage, Claims That Require Verification, Topics to Avoid

**Format requirements:**
- Format: PDF
- Delivery method: Email capture via approved Kit landing page; automated PDF delivery via approved email delivery provider, Resend.
- Length: Short-form (pamphlet, not book)
- Branding: Must use approved brand name "Don't Go Broke" and approved campaign name "Replit – When the Meter Keeps Running"
- Tone: Practical, protective, honest, direct (per Brand Character)
- Claims: Any pricing or credit claims must be verified before inclusion (per Promotion Brief)

**Content requirements (without copy):**
- Must address the core audience problem: hidden compute-time cost accumulation
- Must address all three message pillars: Speed vs. Cost, Visibility Gap, Prevention
- Must address at least the approved objections: manual monitoring, small project size, existing budgeting tools
- Must not contain platform attacks, fear-mongering, guaranteed outcomes, or get-rich claims
- Must not contain outdated or unverified platform pricing

**Review requirements:**
- Stakeholder review and sign-off required
- Legal/Compliance review: all cost-related claims verified before approval
- Design review: layout and branding approved before production

---

### A-02 — Landing Page / Download Flow

**Purpose:** Captures the visitor's email address in exchange for pamphlet delivery. This is the primary lead-capture mechanism for all channels.

**Required inputs:**
- Operations Profile: Existing landing page URL (`https://don-t-go-broke-in-the-age-of-ai.kit.com/65cad4036c`), email provider (Resend), PDF delivery automation status
- Architecture: Section 14 (CTA Role), Section 18 (Success Criteria)
- Promotion Brief: Primary CTA, Secondary CTA

**Format requirements:**
- Must include a functional email sign-up form
- Must trigger automated PDF delivery upon sign-up
- Must be accessible from all approved channels
- Must support UTM or equivalent parameter tracking for channel attribution (per Measurement Setup Requirements in Execution Plan)

**Content requirements (without copy):**
- Must reflect the approved campaign angle and primary message (no copy to be created here)
- Must present the primary CTA: download the free guide
- Must present the secondary CTA: join the email list for future guides
- Must not make unverified claims about platform pricing

**Review requirements:**
- Stakeholder review and sign-off required
- Operations review: confirm form, delivery automation, and tracking are functional before launch
- Privacy/Compliance review: confirm GDPR/CCPA-compliant minimal data collection

### A-03 — Replit Discussion Asset

**Purpose:** Supports the primary discovery channel. Drives cost-aware, community-first engagement in Replit discussion areas. Intended to introduce the pamphlet only when directly relevant.

**Required inputs:**
- Architecture: Section 15 (Channel Roles – Replit as primary discovery)

- Brand Position: Strategic Constraints (never begin with promotion, compete on trust)

**Format requirements:**
- Format: Text post (short-form community post)
- Character or word limit: Must conform to Replit community posting guidelines (to be confirmed during channel readiness check)
- Must not include graphics or attachments unless the platform supports and it adds value

**Content requirements (without copy):**
- Must lead with value to the community, not with promotion
- Must mention the pamphlet only when directly relevant to the discussion
- Must not contain platform attacks, fear-mongering, or spam-like promotional language
- Must align with the approved campaign angle: "The Meter Keeps Running"
- Must reference the landing page URL

**Readiness requirements:**
- Confirmed account access to Replit community
- Confirmed eligibility to post in relevant discussion areas
- Policy compliance check completed

**Review requirements:**
- Stakeholder review and sign-off required
- Legal/Compliance: verify cost-related claim language is accurate and not misleading
