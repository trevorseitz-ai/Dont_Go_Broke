## 3. Schedule Objective

Define the sequence, timing windows, readiness gates, and review checkpoints for publishing all Campaign 1 assets. This plan establishes when each asset is eligible to be published and what conditions must be met before publication — without publishing, posting, editing live systems, writing copy, or launching the campaign.

All timing is expressed in relative days. Day 0 is the scheduled campaign launch day, to be confirmed at the Launch Readiness Review. No calendar dates are used or implied.

## 5. Publishing Readiness Requirements

No asset may be published until all of the following campaign-wide readiness requirements are confirmed. These are prerequisites for Day 0.

| Readiness Requirement | Confirmation Needed |
|----------------------|---------------------|
| All asset production sign-offs complete | Each asset (A-01 through A-07) has individual Stakeholder + applicable reviewer sign-off |
| Tracking confirmed live | A-07 fully operational: UTM parameters active, click tracking confirmed, subscriber tracking confirmed |
| Landing page confirmed live | A-02 functional: sign-up form live, automated delivery via Resend confirmed, UTM tracking confirmed |
| Pamphlet delivery confirmed | A-01 PDF delivered correctly via Resend / Kit automated flow |
| Post-signup email confirmed | A-06 confirmed operational in Resend: triggered on sign-up, correct content, CAN-SPAM / GDPR compliant |
| All channel accounts confirmed | Replit account, Reddit account, X account (if available) confirmed active and posting-eligible |
| Legal/Compliance sign-offs complete | All cost-related claim verifications documented and cleared for all applicable assets |
| Launch Readiness Review complete | Stakeholder reviews and approves the Launch Readiness Report before Day 0 |

If any readiness requirement is unmet, Day 0 must be deferred until it is resolved.

## 10. Review and Pause Criteria

### Standard review checkpoints

Reviews occur at Day -1 (pre-launch), Day 3 (mid-campaign), and Day 7 (post-campaign close). All reviews are observation and decision points — no copy, assets, or live systems are modified during review.

### Pause criteria

The campaign must be paused immediately if any of the following conditions are observed:

| Pause Condition | Required Action |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Tracking is not functioning correctly (no data flowing to measurement systems) | Pause all channel activity; do not publish additional assets until tracking is confirmed operational |
| Landing page sign-up form is non-functional or PDF delivery is failing | Pause channel activity; resolve A-02 and A-06 issues before re-activating |
| A community post receives a policy violation notice or removal | Immediately pause all community channel activity; stakeholder reviews before re-posting or abandoning |
| A published asset is found to contain an unverified or inaccurate claim | Immediately remove or retract the asset; stakeholder and Legal/Compliance review required before replacement |
| Audience feedback indicates fear-based framing or harmful interpretation | Pause; stakeholder reviews the asset against Brand Position constraints |

### Resume criteria

The campaign may resume after a pause only with explicit stakeholder sign-off. No asset may be re-published after a pause without a documented resolution of the pause condition.

## 11. Post-Campaign Review Trigger

The post-campaign review is triggered at Day 7 (or earlier if a pause condition causes early campaign close). The review is conducted as part of the Launch Readiness Report close-out phase.

**Minimum data required for post-campaign review:**

| Metric | Target | Source |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Subscriber sign-ups | Measurable progress toward first 100 | Resend / Kit subscriber list |
| Hypothesis validation signal | ≥ 60 % of feedback prompt responses cite cost-fear as primary concern | A-06 feedback prompt responses |
| Channel effectiveness signal | ≥ 1 channel identified as delivering > 30 % of sign-ups | A-07 UTM attribution data |
| Community engagement | Volume of replies and comments logged across A-03, A-04, A-05 | Manual community log |

**Review output:** A documented set of findings — what worked, what did not, what was confirmed or disconfirmed about the campaign hypothesis. These findings become inputs to Campaign 2 planning.
