## 3. Production Objective

Define how each approved Campaign 1 asset draft will be converted into a production-ready asset — with all quality checks, claim verifications, design verifications, delivery verifications, and tracking verifications completed — before any asset advances to the Publishing Schedule.

This plan defines what production tasks must be completed, in what order, with what acceptance criteria, and through what approval gates, before any asset is considered production-ready. This plan does not produce any asset, file, or live-system change.

## 5. Production Readiness Criteria

Before any asset advances to the Asset Production stage, it must satisfy all of the following readiness criteria. These are entry gates — not exit conditions.

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Stakeholder approval of draft | The asset draft must be individually signed off by Trevor Seitz before production begins |
| Legal/Compliance approval of draft | All cost-related claims in the draft must be verified and cleared |
| Design approval (A-01, A-02 only) | Brand color palette, typography, logo, and layout must be approved |
| Operations approval (A-02, A-06, A-07) | Form, delivery automation, and tracking must be confirmed functional |
| Channel readiness (A-03, A-04, A-05) | Account access, posting eligibility, and policy compliance must be confirmed |
| Claim verification documentation | A claim verification document must accompany each applicable draft |

No asset may enter production if any applicable readiness criterion is unmet.

| Stage | Asset(s) | Production Focus | Gate Before Advancing |

## 7. Per-Asset Production Requirements

### A-07 — Analytics / Tracking Setup (Production Stage 1)

**Production tasks:**
1. Implement the approved UTM parameter structure across all four channel URLs.
2. Confirm click-tracking is live on the Kit landing page.
3. Confirm subscriber sign-up tracking is active in the Resend / Kit system.
4. Confirm the manual community engagement log is prepared and accessible.
5. Confirm the hypothesis-validation feedback capture method is operational in A-06.
6. Conduct a full tracking verification pass (see Section 11).
7. Document the live tracking configuration for stakeholder review.

**Production output:** A documented tracking configuration record confirming all measurement systems are live and operational. No file, graphic, or copy produced.

**Required sign-off before advancing:** Operations review + Stakeholder sign-off.

---

### A-02 — Landing Page / Download Flow (Production Stage 2)

**Production tasks:**
1. Verify that all gaps identified in the Development Plan A-02 audit have been resolved.
2. Confirm the email sign-up form is live and submits correctly.
3. Confirm automated PDF delivery via Resend triggers on form submission.
4. Confirm UTM parameter tracking is active for all four channels.
5. Confirm the landing page reflects the approved campaign angle and primary message (verified against the signed-off draft — no new copy written here).
6. Confirm GDPR/CCPA-compliant data collection is in place and verified.
7. Conduct a delivery and access verification pass (see Section 12).
8. Document the production-ready status of the landing page for stakeholder review.

**Production output:** A documented landing page production-readiness record. No landing page edits, copy changes, or live-system modifications are made in this plan.

**Required sign-off before advancing:** Operations review + Stakeholder sign-off.

---

### A-01 — Replit Pamphlet (Production Stage 3)

**Production tasks:**
1. Confirm the signed-off pamphlet draft and claim verification document are in hand.
2. Apply the approved brand identity: logo, color palette, and typography (per Design approval from Development Plan).
3. Format the pamphlet to the approved PDF layout: readable, clean, short-form pamphlet.
4. Export the final pamphlet as a production-ready PDF file.
5. Store the PDF at an agreed project file location accessible for Resend delivery configuration.
6. Confirm the PDF is deliverable via the Resend / Kit automated delivery flow.
7. Conduct a claim verification check on the final PDF (see Section 9).
8. Conduct a design and brand verification check on the final PDF (see Section 10).
9. Conduct a quality assurance pass (see Section 8).
10. Submit the final PDF for Stakeholder, Legal/Compliance, and Design sign-off.

**Production output:** A production-ready PDF file stored at the agreed project location, confirmed deliverable via Resend. No PDF is created or attached to this plan.

**Required sign-off before advancing:** Stakeholder + Legal/Compliance + Design review sign-off on the final PDF.

## 9. Claim Verification Requirements

A final claim verification check must be performed on the production-ready version of each applicable asset, even if a claim verification pass was completed during Asset Development. This ensures no new unverified claims were introduced during production formatting or editing.

**Assets requiring final claim verification:** A-01, A-03, A-04, A-05

**Final verification requirements:**
- All references to Replit credit costs, usage meter behavior, or billing mechanics must be confirmed current against Replit documentation at the time of production sign-off.
- Any claim that has become unverifiable since the Development Plan verification must be removed or reworded before production sign-off.
- The source and date of each verified claim must be documented in the production sign-off submission.

**Completion condition:** A final claim verification record accompanies each applicable asset at production sign-off. No asset containing unverified claims may advance to the Publishing Schedule.

## 13. Review and Approval Gates

Every asset must pass all applicable review gates before it is considered production-ready and eligible for the Publishing Schedule.

| Gate | Required For | Trigger |

**Gate sequencing rule:** All applicable gates must be cleared for a given asset before that asset is handed off to the Publishing Schedule. A single uncleared gate blocks the asset from advancing.

## 14. Production Risks

| Risk | Asset(s) Affected | Mitigation |
|------|------------------|------------|
| Replit pricing or credit information has changed since Development Plan claim verification | A-01, A-03, A-04, A-05 | Final claim verification (Section 9) is required at production sign-off, regardless of Development Plan verification status |
| PDF export introduces formatting errors or unverified content | A-01 | Design review of the final exported PDF is required before sign-off; no draft content may carry through |
| Resend delivery automation is not correctly configured for the final PDF | A-01, A-06 | Delivery verification (Section 12) must confirm delivery before production sign-off; testing is required |
| Kit landing page UTM parameters not correctly implemented | A-02, A-07 | Tracking verification (Section 11) must be completed and documented before any channel asset advances |
| Reddit subreddit posting rules changed since Development Plan channel readiness check | A-04 | Re-confirm posting eligibility at production stage before finalizing the asset |
| X account creation is still pending | A-05 | A-05 is the last production stage; if X account is not live at production time, stakeholder decides whether to defer A-05 or proceed with Stages 1–6 only |
| All production sign-offs cannot be completed before campaign launch target | All | Stakeholder decision required: minimum viable production set is A-07, A-02, A-01, A-06; community assets (A-03, A-04, A-05) may be staged for a subsequent release if approved |
