# **Don't Go Broke Promotion Strategy v2.2**

## **Status**

Approved

## **Audience Factory Validation Project**

---

# **Executive Summary**

The primary objective of the Don't Go Broke project is no longer simply acquiring subscribers.

The primary objective is validating Audience Factory through a series of controlled publishing experiments.

Each pamphlet represents a separate audience hypothesis.

Each release provides an opportunity to:

* Test messaging  
* Test acquisition channels  
* Test conversion behavior  
* Test onboarding  
* Test subscriber feedback collection  
* Improve Audience Factory processes

Rather than launching a bundle immediately, the project will release pamphlets individually.

After sufficient learning has been collected from the individual releases, a bundled offer will be launched and compared against the individual pamphlets.

The goal is not virality.

The goal is repeatable audience acquisition combined with measurable learning.

---

# **Strategic Objective**

Validate Audience Factory by acquiring subscribers through a sequence of controlled publishing experiments.

Primary Objectives:

* Acquire the first 100 subscribers  
* Validate Audience Factory processes  
* Compare pamphlet performance  
* Identify the strongest audience problems  
* Identify repeatable acquisition channels

Secondary Objectives:

* Gather subscriber feedback  
* Refine messaging  
* Validate future guide demand  
* Build the foundation of the Don't Go Broke publishing business

---

# **Core Strategic Insight**

The project contains multiple hypotheses.

Each pamphlet represents a different beginner problem.

Audience Factory gains more useful learning by testing these problems individually than by bundling them immediately.

Therefore:

Individual pamphlet releases will occur before bundle launch.

---

# **Audience**

Primary Audience:

First-time AI builders.

Characteristics:

* Budget constrained  
* Inexperienced  
* Optimistic  
* Moving quickly  
* Using AI-assisted development tools

---

# **Positioning**

The practical survival guide for first-time AI builders.

Core belief:

Build with your eyes open.

---

# **Acquisition Philosophy**

Meet builders where they already are.

Prioritize:

* Reddit  
* Replit discussions  
* AI builder communities  
* Indie Hacker discussions

Use social platforms primarily for credibility and content distribution.

---

# **Community Participation Rules**

The objective is to become useful before becoming visible.

Guidelines:

* Answer questions first  
* Lead with value  
* Never begin with promotion  
* Only share pamphlets when directly relevant  
* Maintain a minimum ratio of five helpful contributions for every promotional mention

Trust accumulation is more valuable than short-term traffic.

---

# **Launch Structure**

## **Phase 1 — Individual Validation**

Each release is a standalone experiment.

Measure:

* Subscriber acquisition  
* Conversion rate  
* Engagement

* Message resonance

---

## **Phase 2 — Bundle Validation**

After all individual pamphlets have been released:

* Bundle subscriber quality
