# Executive Summary

Don't Go Broke is not attempting to launch a large publishing brand immediately.

The objective is to validate Audience Factory while simultaneously building the foundation of a long-term publishing business.

The project already possesses:

* Domain
* Landing page
* Email delivery
* Lead magnet
* Brand position
* Initial content

The project does not possess:

* Audience
* Social presence
* Community presence
* Analytics history
* Subscriber feedback

The strategy therefore focuses on:

Acquire First Subscribers
→ Gather Feedback
→ Learn What Resonates
→ Improve Messaging
→ Validate Book Demand
→ Improve Audience Factory

The goal is not virality.

The goal is repeatable audience acquisition combined with measurable learning.

---

# Strategic Objective

We will validate Audience Factory by building the strongest possible foundation for the Don't Go Broke publishing business.

Primary Objective:

* Acquire the first 100 subscribers as quickly as possible.

Those subscribers will be used to:

* Learn which messages resonate with first-time AI builders
* Identify effective acquisition channels
* Gather audience feedback
* Validate demand for future guides and books
* Improve Audience Factory processes

Campaign-level milestones may be smaller, but all campaigns contribute toward the first 100-subscriber objective.

# Acquisition Strategy

## Principle

Start where the audience already exists.

Do not attempt to create new communities.

Do not attempt to manufacture demand.

Join existing conversations where practical.

---

# Acquisition Sources

## Tier 1

Highest Priority:

* Reddit
* AI builder communities
* Replit discussions
* Indie Hacker discussions

Reason:

People are already discussing costs, usage, mistakes, deployment issues, and beginner experiences.

## Tier 2

Medium Priority:

* X
* LinkedIn

Reason:

Useful for credibility, discoverability, and repeatable publishing, but no existing audience should be assumed.

## Tier 3

Low Priority:

* YouTube
* Discord
* Blog

Reason:

Too resource intensive for the current stage unless automation or reusable long-form content makes them practical.

---

# Channel Strategy

## Primary Channel

Reddit and community-based discovery.

Goal:

Answer questions and observe audience language.

Not:

Promote pamphlets.

The guide should appear naturally only when relevant.

### Community Participation Rule

The objective is to become useful before becoming visible.

Guidelines:

* Answer questions first
* Contribute helpful information before sharing links
* Maintain a minimum ratio of five helpful contributions for every promotional mention
* Only mention the pamphlet when it directly helps answer the discussion
* Never lead with promotion

Trust accumulation is more valuable than short-term traffic.

---

## Secondary Channel

X

Goal:

Publish observations, lessons learned, and cost-awareness warnings.

Frequency:

3–4 posts per week if automation-supported and approved.

---

## Tertiary Channel

LinkedIn

Goal:

Professional credibility and founder-style publishing.

Frequency:

1–2 posts per week if a company or founder-compatible presence is created.

# Feedback Collection Strategy

The purpose of early subscriber acquisition is not only growth.

It is also learning.

Every meaningful subscriber interaction should be categorized when practical.

Track:

* Questions asked
* Problems experienced
* Platform mentioned
* Purchase intent signals
* Future guide requests
* Common language used by readers

These insights become inputs for:

* Future pamphlets
* Future books
* Message refinement
* Campaign optimization
* Audience Factory improvements

The objective is to systematically convert audience feedback into publishing and marketing intelligence.

---

# Learning Objectives

# Audience Factory Validation Criteria

Audience Factory will be considered successfully validated when:

* The first 100 subscribers are acquired
* Acquisition sources can be identified
* At least one repeatable acquisition channel is demonstrated
* Audience language patterns are documented
* Future guide demand becomes observable
* Feedback collection processes produce actionable insights

The objective is not simply audience growth.

The objective is repeatable audience growth combined with measurable learning.
