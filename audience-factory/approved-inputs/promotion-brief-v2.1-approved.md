# Product Overview

## Product Description

Don't Go Broke is an educational publishing series for first-time AI builders using AI-powered development platforms. It helps solo builders understand costs, limits, risks, and tradeoffs before mistakes become expensive.

## Problem It Solves

First-time builders can build quickly with AI platforms but often do not understand how credits, usage, deployment, hosting, limits, and hidden costs work.

## What Happens If The Problem Is Ignored?

Builders may waste money, overbuild before validating, misunderstand platform limits, or receive unexpected bills.

## Desired Customer Outcome

Readers build with realistic expectations, practical knowledge, and greater confidence.

## Unique Advantage

Practical, short, warning-oriented guides for real beginner mistakes without hype, platform attacks, or business coaching.

## Proof / Evidence

- Replit pamphlet created
- Additional pamphlet concepts created
- User experience and research-driven editorial process
- Audience Factory planning and validation work
