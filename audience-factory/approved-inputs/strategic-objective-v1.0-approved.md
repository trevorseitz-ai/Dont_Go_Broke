# Don't Go Broke Strategic Objective v1.0

## Status

Approved

## Strategic Objective

We will validate the Audience Factory by building the strongest possible foundation for the Don't Go Broke publishing business.

That means acquiring the first 100 subscribers as quickly as possible.

We will use those subscribers to learn which messages resonate with first-time AI builders and to gather evidence that readers are willing to purchase future books and guides.

## Success Definition

Success is not measured solely by subscriber count.

Success is measured by proving that the Audience Factory can repeatedly:

- Attract attention
- Generate subscribers
- Capture audience feedback
- Validate demand
- Produce actionable marketing insights
- Support future publishing products

## Objective Hierarchy

1. Validate Audience Factory
2. Build the strongest possible publishing foundation
3. Get the first 100 subscribers
4. Validate future book demand
5. Learn what messaging resonates
