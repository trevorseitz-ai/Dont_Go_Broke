# Implementation Plan

## Goal Description
Produce the final manuscript for the "Before You Trust the First Draft" booklet, completing the sequence of documents up to `05-final-copy.md` autonomously according to the Autonomous Booklet Production Standard. The product aims to help first-time AI builders review AI-generated code before trusting it. 

## Process Overview
1.  **Source Review:** Check `publication-concept.md` and `publication-brief.md` for clarity and consistency.
2.  **Outline:** Map the required 10 sections into a structural outline.
3.  **Section Plan:** Detail the takeaways, purpose, and content focus for each section.
4.  **Draft Copy:** Write the manuscript following the protective, practical, and beginner-safe tone.
5.  **Editorial Review:** Verify alignment with source materials and standards.
6.  **Final Copy:** Deliver a clean final version for human review.

## Constraints & Gates
- Maintain a platform-independent approach.
- Ensure the booklet does not cross into build planning (booklet 2) or post-launch ops (booklet 4).
- Internal quality gates are executed autonomously without human approval until the final manuscript.
