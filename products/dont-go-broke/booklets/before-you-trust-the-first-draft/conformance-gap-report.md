## Conformance Gaps

### Layout Gaps
None. The CSS reconciliation successfully applied the US Letter Payhip geometry.

### Density Gaps
The average word count per full text page (259.3) falls far short of the 320 target. This is the direct result of 26 hard page breaks generated by the H2s, creating significant artificial whitespace at the end of nearly every page.

### Heading Structure Gaps
The booklet is the most structurally fragmented in the collection. With 26 H2s (24 numbered chapters), it exceeds the maximum 7-chapter limit by a factor of three. Sub-tests, workflows, protocols, checklists, and procedural steps have been improperly elevated to H2 status, destroying the chapter rhythm.

### Art Rhythm Gaps
The booklet completely lacks the required 6 internal art insertions / placeholders.

### Field Note / Set-Off Text Gaps
While Field Notes are present, 8 distinct tools (tests, protocols, worksheets, receipts) are mixed directly into the H2 flow rather than isolated in Back Matter or formatted as set-off blocks.

### Page Count Gaps
Because of the 26 hard page breaks, the physical page count is heavily inflated with white space.

## Manuscript / Structure Implications
To conform to the standard, this booklet requires a severe structural compression pass without deleting instructional content. Likely restructuring patterns include:
- **Massive H2 Consolidation:** Grouping related tests (e.g., "5. The 'Looks Done vs. Is Done' Test", "6. The Original Intent Check", "9. The Data and Memory Check") under a single parent chapter (e.g., "The Data Review Protocol").
- **Demoting Protocols to H3s:** Procedural walkthroughs and checklists must become H3s nested under their parent concepts.
- **Back Matter Expansion:** Relocating the "First Draft Testing Worksheet", "Decision Guide", "Confidence Receipt", and "Safe Approval Protocol" into a dedicated Back Matter section.
- **Establishing Art Rhythm:** 6 `[ILLUSTRATION PLACEHOLDER: ...]` blocks must be injected.
