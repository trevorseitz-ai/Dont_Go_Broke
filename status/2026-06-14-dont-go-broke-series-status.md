# Don't Go Broke Series — Project Status Report

**Date:** June 14, 2026

## Executive Summary

The Don't Go Broke series has evolved from a single Replit guide into a broader publishing initiative focused on helping first-time AI builders avoid expensive mistakes when using AI-powered development platforms.

The project now has a defined brand identity, a working publishing pipeline, a lead-generation system, and the first title approaching production readiness.

Current status: **Series Foundation Complete / First Product Near Launch**

---

# Project Overview

**Series Name:**
Don't Go Broke in the Age of AI

**Positioning:**
Short, practical survival guides for solo builders and first-time founders using AI development platforms.

**Target Audience:**

* First-time AI app builders
* Solo founders
* Students and hobbyists
* Budget-conscious developers
* Users building their first commercial product

**Core Promise:**
Help builders avoid hidden costs, common beginner mistakes, and expensive platform-specific traps before they burn through credits, subscriptions, or infrastructure budgets.
